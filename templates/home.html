Focus Nowâ€™s goal is to provide a place for users to easily manage their work with a customizable timer. The intention of this app is to help users maintain a balance between their focus and break time in order to avoid constant distractions while they study, code, etc. The goal of Focus Now is to help students excel in time management with an alternative time management method.
By splitting your time into 30 minute cycles of 25 minutes of focus and 5 minutes of break, you can maintain a good balance between your time. Study sessions don't feel like they drag on endlessly, and you have something to break you out of a sluggish break.
Focus Now provides a scheduling function where you can log tasks you need to accomplish and the approximate time each task would take. Focus Now then automatically splits the times into the cycles so you can productively get your work done.
Instructions
  1. Use the Create Task button to add a new task, along with the approximate time it would take as well as its priority.
  2. These tasks are automatically sorted by priority and added to the Schedule tab.
  3. Click the schedule tab to see added tasks, as well as edit and delete them as needed.
  4. Start the timer. The timer is automatically made to cycle through focus and breaks.
By default, Focus Now cycles are split into 25 minute focus sessions, 5 minute short break sessions (breaks while completing a task), and 10 minute long break sessions (breaks between tasks). If you feel you would like to adjust these timings, go to the Settings page and change the values as you would like.
